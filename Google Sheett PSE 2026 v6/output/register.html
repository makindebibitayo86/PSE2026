Registration Guide
Read carefully before proceeding to the Register tab.
Registration Steps
1
Personal Information

Enter your name, phone and LGA exactly as they appear in your official civil service records.

2
Ministry & Department

Select your Ministry first, then your Department or Agency. Contact your HR unit if unsure.

3
Grade Level

Select your current confirmed grade level as per your appointment or last promotion letter.

4
Payment & Papers

Enter your Bank Transaction Reference number, select amount paid and choose your papers.

5
Declaration

Read and accept the statutory declaration. False information is a civil service offence.

6
Exam Slip

Print immediately after registration. No slip means no entry on examination day.

Duplicate registrations using the same phone number will be rejected automatically.
Payment must be made to the designated Oyo State Government Bank account. The Transaction Reference (Tran Ref) is printed on your bank teller receipt or included in your transfer notification.

5 Statutory Declaration

I, [ your full name will appear here ], hereby solemnly declare that:

  1. All information provided in this registration form is true, complete and accurate to the best of my knowledge and belief.
  2. I am a serving officer in the Oyo State Civil Service and I am duly eligible to sit for this examination.
  3. The Bank Transaction Reference entered is valid and represents a payment made by me or on my behalf solely for the purpose of this examination.
  4. I understand that any false, misleading or fraudulent information provided herein shall render my registration void, result in immediate disqualification, and constitute a civil service offence liable to disciplinary proceedings under the Public Service Rules of Oyo State.
  5. I consent to the processing of my personal data by the Office of the Head of Service, Oyo State, for the purpose of administering this examination and communicating results to the relevant Ministry, Department or Agency.
You must accept this declaration before you can submit your registration.
